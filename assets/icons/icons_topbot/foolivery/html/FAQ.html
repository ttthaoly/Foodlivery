How to Determine for yourself the calorie level ?

Determine for yourself the calorie level at which you are losing weight or getting better (each has his own, and there are many methods for calculating this figure), and try to compose your daily menu at least approximately within this calorie level. The label of any product today shows its nutritional value and calorie content, and you can always calculate the calories consumed.
